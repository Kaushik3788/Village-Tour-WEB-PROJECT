***TOURISM***

Bihar is home to many tourist attractions and is visited by large numbers of tourists from all over the world. Around total 6 million tourists visit Bihar every year.



  • Kesariya Stupa

    • The stupa is said to be the tallest and the largest Buddhist stupa in the world.



  • Someshwar Shiv Mandir, Areraj

    • Someshwar Nath Mahadev Mandir is an age-old temple located in Aeraj. The major deity of the holy temple is of Lord Shiva.
