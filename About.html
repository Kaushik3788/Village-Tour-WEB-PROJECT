***About My Village***

Bankat is a small Village in Kesaria Block in Purbi Champaran District of Bihar State, India. It comes under Bankat Panchayath. It belongs to Tirhut Division . It is located 37 KM towards South from District head quarters Motihari. 6 KM from Kesaria. 96 KM from State capital Patna. Bankat Pin code is 845424 and postal head office is Kesharia . Tajpur Pathkauliya (6 KM) , Dilawarpur ( 7 KM ) , Darmaha ( 7 KM ) , Rajpur ( 7 KM ) , Dhekahan ( 9 KM ) are the nearby Villages to Bankat. Bankat is surrounded by Sahebganj Block towards South , Kalyanpur Block towards North , Baikunthpur Block towards west , Chakia (Pipra) Block towards East . Motipur , Barauli , Areraj , Motihari are the near by Cities to Bankat. This Place is in the border of the Purbi Champaran District and Muzaffarpur District. Muzaffarpur District Sahebganj is South towards this place . Also it is in the Border of other district Gopalganj .
